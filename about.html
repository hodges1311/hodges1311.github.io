What is FlyBy?

We offer a simple, easy-to-use platform for the buying and selling of paper aircraft designs. FlyBy strives to simultaneously protect our user's intellectual rights and to facilitate their product distribution.

Our Mission Statement

Here at FlyBy, we want encourage creativity and community by allowing the continued creation of high quality paper planes. From enthusiasts to young children and passersby, FlyBy is meant to be accessible for everyone and tries to offer a wide variety of crafts

Buying and Selling

All users are able to submit designs through our website and able to fully control their product's prices. Addtionally, by utilitizing our website we can assisst you in protecting your intellectual rights.
